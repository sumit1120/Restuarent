About Us

We've beem making healthy food last for 10 years

Founded in 2000 down a crowded lane in old Mumbai, evolved over time – the large, first-floor hall is now separated into small rooms and air conditioning has been installed – but what hasn’t changed in over 20 years is the quality of the food, all of it vegetarian and served up with the charm that comes from the restaurant still being family-owned. Huge steel thali plates are the name of the game, bearing dinky little pots of chutneys, dal, farsan, curries and samosas to be scooped up with roti, puri and bhakri. Expect to queue at the weekend, which is no surprise given the excellent value for money. Go on an empty stomach – portions are huge.
